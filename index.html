About Sociovation

Sociovation is a youth based organization which is working on various problems faced by people in today’s world using creative and innovative ways. It works to help underprivileged children, challenges faced by women, children education and raft of other issues.

We work among various sectors along with many people, projects and organizations like LECIN, Sulabh International, Project WHY and many more.

We believe in creation, development, innovation, adoption and integration of new concepts and practices to bring changes in various fields.

How We WorkOur Work

We work to bring changes in the society through innovative methods and innovative minds. We want to provide a platform for those who have eagerness to learn new things. We try to make today’s generation more independent and responsible such that they can tackle the challenges of life.

Children Women Environment

Child Education

“Every child is different and it’s the fundamental right of every child to get education”-believing on the same ideology, the centre for social innovation chooses “CHILD EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMENT” as one of the major area to work upon. We are trying to create a platform for all the children who belong to underprivileged sections of society and have a desire to learn but are unable to get the resources needed to learn. We are trying to educate them and to bring a smile on their face.

Women Empowerment

The empowered woman is powerful beyond measures and beautiful beyond description. We have a mission to provide resource poor women with knowledge and skills so that they can excel as professionals and earn a “livelihood with dignity and respect”. We wish to open gates for them into jobs and markets that are presently closed for them.
We want to create a safe and respectful working environment for them.

Cleanliness and Environment

The centre for social innovation tries to put forth before society its views and style of working. We keep our homes clean and throw the garbage, spit betel, pee or even defecate whenever we get a chance. Workshops and visits are done to different places like Sulabh international museum of toilets and many others. Tree plantation drives are done by the centre in order to make our contribution towards keeping India clean and green.

Our Wonderful Team

A team of creative and enthusiastic students united from different fields, working together to fulfill a common dream of a well formed society. Though the core-team has twelve members, we have more students from our college and even more people volunteering in our various programs.
