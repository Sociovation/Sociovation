Women’s Voluntary Organisation

WOMEN’S VOLUNTARY ORGANISATION

The Women's Voluntary Organisation was registered in the year 1992 under the Manipur Societies Registration Act, 1989 with a motto to generate sustainable employment for the educated youth and poor household women on their livelihood and socio-economic condition with profuse yearning and advocating humanity. WVO is a Non-profit making, Non-governmental, Non sectarian and Non Political organisation having objective of multi sectoral development without discrimination of any caste, creed and religion since its inception.
WVO is working with "Working Children", "Women Empowerment", "Swadhar Home & Helpline" "Creche Centre" to bring peace and harmony in the present society of Manipur and also working as Micro-finance Institution for economic upliftment of the rural masses.
