Saubhagya Foundation

SAUBHAGYA FOUNDATION

Saubhagya Foundation was founded on 07 April 2018 under the society registration Act 1860. The aim of the society is to work and organise various kinds of programmes for the development and welfare of the society.

AIM :

The aim of the society is to work and organise various programmes fo the development and welfare of the society.

VISION :

To impact primary literacy and basic education to children, underprivileged and weaker section of the society to aim towards higher literacy levels across the country by running educational schools, colleges, degree colleges, institutions, vocational training centers.

Project WHY

PROJECT WHY

Project WHY is a flagship program of Sri Ram Goburdhun Charitable Trust, that sets out to create spaces where by quality educational support to underprivileged children can improve their learning outcomes. It is an after-school support program that goes beyond academics to include life skills and all-round development.

AIM :

It aims to provide life skills and all round development that goes beyond academics.

VISION :

Project Why’s vision of a desirable world is where all children have quality education and are able to complete their schooling years. Where all the children have the support to build their skills and have a voice. We also believe in gender justice and self-reliance in improving socio-economic status through skill enhancement.

Hands for Help

HANDS FOR HELP

"HANDS FOR HELP" was registered as societies registration Act in Delhi/NCT, India, to start its vital work on24TH August 2015. We strongly believe in the active participation of children, and thus started working with them from 2015 onwards to address key child rights issues such as survival, protection, development and participation.

AIM :

The society works in the field of facilitating better quality of life through community development, child welfare, education, and per the objectives.

VISION :

The mission is to provide childhood empowerment through training and action, with a special focus on street and working children and especially girls, to ensure their rights.

Chhoti Chhoti Khushiyan

CHHOTI CHHOTI KHUSHIYAN

This charity foundation started informally back in September but got registered in June 2016. In 2015, on a routine visit to an orphanage, Dr Varun was moved when one child mentioned that he’s never had his birthday celebrated. He could only imagine what their joy would be to have theirs celebrated at least once. Thus was born the idea of setting up “Chhoti Chhoti Khushiyan”, meaning joys from the trivial things.

AIM :

The NGO aims at supporting children through the involvment of the care given to them and the quality of their life in general.

VISION :

To be the most effective, approachable and sustainable children’s charity that we can be committed to supporting the children through the improvement of the care given to them and the quality of their lives in general.

Sakaar Outreach

SAKAAR OUTREACH

Sakaar Outreach is a registered not-for- profit organization working for the cause and concern of the communities, those are in need. The word ‘Sakaar’ for us translates to providing a helping hand to the lesser-privileged by realizing their potential and equipping them with the skills required to enable them to stand on their own feet.

AIM :

It aims to improve the living conditions of society in rural, semi-urban areas through the medium of architecture.

VISION :

The foundation completed a large number of projects which cover an impressive range from religious, educational, housing, public, health care, commercial, urban designing and landscaping schemes.
To achieve these objectives, the Foundation has brought out three books, prepared, participated and organized various seminars.

Jazbaa Foundation

JAZBAA FOUNDATION

Jazbaa Foundation is a non-governmental and non-profit making organization (NGO) based and working in Delhi, NCR. The journey of the organization began in 2017.

AIM :

Jazbaa Foundation aims to provide education to the under privileged children, to build better livelihood opportunities for the rural and underprivileged urban youth, to enrich quality health services for adolescent girls and women.

VISION :

The thought process behind Jazbaa Foundation is that quality education is the only medium that can improve the living standards of the poor people. It is the most powerful tool, which can equip them to make informed and intelligent choices about lives and future.
Jazbaa Foundation believes that education is the only weapon to fight poverty and truly empower the children who tomorrow through the medium of education and unleash the power of knowledge that is inaccessible to millions.
